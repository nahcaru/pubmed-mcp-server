# 2.9.8 — 2026-06-30

## Added

- **`pubmed_fetch_fulltext` section-filter-miss notice** — when a `sections` filter removes every body section from an article that had sections upstream, the tool surfaces a recovery notice via a new `enrichment.notice` block (emitted through `ctx.enrich.notice`) instead of silently returning an empty body. The notice names the requested section terms and the affected article id(s), distinguishes a filtered-empty body from one absent upstream, and suggests retrying without `sections` or filtering on broader headings (Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion). The article is still returned with its empty body — not an error — and the miss is detected on both the PMC EFetch and Europe PMC paths. ([#80](https://github.com/cyanheads/pubmed-mcp-server/issues/80))

## Dependencies

- `@cyanheads/mcp-ts-core` ^0.10.9 → ^0.10.10 (framework js-yaml v4→v5 migration and biome 2.5.1; no consumer change — this server installs no js-yaml directly)
